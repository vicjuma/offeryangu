How it Works

For Buyers

  1. Use the search panel to find an item on offer

    Find what you need using search panel and apply relevant filters. We have thousands of adverts, browse through and choose precisely what you are looking for.

  2. Contact the seller

    You may use chat on OfferYangu or call them via phone. Discuss all the details and reach an agreement regarding the item or service on offer.

  3. Collect your item or request a delivery

    Meet the seller in public place and be sure to pay only after collecting and inspecting the item.

  4. Leave your feedback by providing a genuine review about the offer and seller

    Feel free to tell us about your purchase. Your review will be published online on the seller’s page and will be very helpful for other buyers. Let’s cultivate and promote a vibrant bargain hunters movement together!

Safety Tips for Buyers

  • Do not pay in advance (except to OfferYangu Business Till).
  • Try to meet at a safe, public location
  • Check the item on offer BEFORE you buy it
  • Pay only after collecting and inspecting the item
  • Never give out your financial information, including mpesa details
